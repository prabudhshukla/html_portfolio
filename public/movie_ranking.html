Top 3 Movies of All Time

Baahubali

Baahubali: The Beginning is a 2015 Indian epic action film directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It was produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under their banner Arka Media Works. The film stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah, Ramya Krishnan, Sathyaraj, Nassar, and Sudeep in the lead roles. The film is the first of two cinematic parts and was followed by Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017). The story is set in medieval India and follows the conflict between two brothers in the fictional kingdom of Mahishmati. The film was made on a budget of ₹180 crore (US$25 million) and was released on 10 July 2015. It received critical acclaim for its visual effects, action sequences, and performances, particularly those of Prabhas and Rana Daggubati. The film was a commercial success, grossing over ₹650 crore (US$91 million) worldwide, making it the highest-grossing Indian film at the time of its release. Baahubali: The Beginning won several awards, including the National Film Award for Best Feature Film and the Filmfare Award for Best Film – Telugu.

Dhurandhar

Dhurandhar is a 2022 Indian Kannada-language action film directed by Arjun Janya. The film stars Chiranjeevi Sarja, Deepti Sati, and Aditi Prabhudeva in the lead roles. The story revolves around a police officer who takes on a powerful criminal organization to bring justice to the city. The film features high-octane action sequences and a gripping storyline that keeps the audience engaged throughout. Dhurandhar received positive reviews for its performances, direction, and action choreography, making it one of the notable films in the Kannada film industry in 2022.
